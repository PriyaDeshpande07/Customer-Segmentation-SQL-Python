CUSTOMER SEGMENTATION ANALYSIS – SQL OUTPUTS

1. Average Salary (Aggregation)

SQL Query:
SELECT AVG(SAL)
FROM EMP;

Output:

## AVG(SAL)

2073.21429

2. Average Salary by Department (GROUP BY)

SQL Query:
SELECT DEPTNO, AVG(SAL)
FROM EMP
GROUP BY DEPTNO;

Output:

DEPTNO   AVG(SAL)

---

30       1566.66667
20       2175
10       2916.66667

3. Employee Count by Department (COUNT + GROUP BY)

SQL Query:
SELECT DEPTNO, COUNT(*)
FROM EMP
GROUP BY DEPTNO;

Output:

DEPTNO   COUNT(*)

---

30       6
20       5
10       3
